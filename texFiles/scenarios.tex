\subsection*{Scenario 2 - Connor Byrne, Department of Science Lecturer}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Scenario 2 - Connor Byrne}
One module that Byrne teaches is structured to have many small bi-weekly Mulitple Choice Question quizzes based on material and topics covered over the weeks. Byrne is preparing teaching materials for this module at the beginning of the semester but has previously found it difficult to be consistent with reminders about the quizzes as announcements across Moodle, email, and in-class reminders were often missed or overlooked.\\

Using SETUP, Byrne can schedule all the quizzes through the built in academic calender, which will automatically sync with students personal timetables. He can choose for these quizzes to send push notification alerts to the students if it is a certain amount of time before each quiz, for example, 2 days and 24 hours before.\\

Byrne can also contact students easier through SETUP and stay consistently in touch with any questions or queries about the MCQ without needing to check multiple platforms. Students can view the quiz details, deadline, study material, and any feedback after the quiz. \\

This helps Byrne improve student engagement and fewer missed assesments, but also allows him to spend less time sending repeated reminders or responding to questions across multiple platforms which allows him more focus on teaching and research.\\
