\subsection*{Scenario 3}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Scenario 3}

Mario Angelo is a 46 year old mother of two daughters. She works as a nurse and is also a mature student in SETU. 
She's always busy every weekday dropping her daughters to and from school, going to her classes midday and then working in the evenings. 
She loves being occupied but she lacks time management. Sometimes, it's difficult for her to keep on track when she needs to submit her assignments 
and would miss the deadlines. Since Maria and her husband alternate times to pick up their daughters, Maria would forget and her husband would remind her, leaving 
her daughters waiting more outside of school hours or either making them late to school by a minute. This also affects Maria by being late to her own class in the mornings.\\

With the help of SETUP, she can benefit from the app by scheduling her day. The To-Do and Notes Tool icon, she can track her deadlines, and jot down notes to write before her day starts. 
The app lets you add a template to add schedules and times. This is easy for Maria, so she knows what time of the day to get things done.\\

If she misses a deadline, SETUP has an email integration where it allows the user to connect to several email accounts to one inbox. It enables her to 
receive notifications from that application. It supports her so she doesn't have to go from app to app.\\

This improves Maria's organising skills. Now she doesn't need to remind herself but with the support of the application, it lets her keep on track. She is able to send emails to lecturers just incase 
she needs to have extensions and also receive the email all in one application.
