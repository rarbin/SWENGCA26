\section*{5. Prototype}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{5. Prototype}


\subsection*{Definition}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Definition}
A prototype is an early example or model of a product used to view and test visual concepts, processes, and designs.

\subsection*{Tools Used}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{Tools Used}
 To build our prototype, Canva was used. Canva is very simple to use and has a beginner-friendly set up and layout of features making it easy to work quickly and efficiently. There are also a lot of free sources on the platform that allow for easy insert into designs and layouts. Overall, we decided to pull the colour theme from SETU itself and overall, it was fun to experiment with the layouts, colours, designs, fonts, and anything else that went into the process of making the prototype.
