\subsection*{User Story 1}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{User Story 1}
\textbf{As a Lecturer who also works in research groups within the university, I want:}
\begin{itemize}
  \item To be able to quickly contact or jump between conversations with students and with the research group.
  \item To provide fast information to students about assesments, deadlines, changes to timetables, etc.
  \item To easily share resources such as lecture notes, links to resources,  file uploads, etc, with other peeople on the platform.
  \item To manage all communication in a clean and efficient manner to keep in contact and up-to-date.
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{User Story 2}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{User Story 2}
\textbf{As a department staff member who helps moderate the platform, I want:}
\begin{itemize}
    \item To manage and update university-wide announcemnts, events, important notices, etc.
    \item To have the ability to help solve user-realted issues, such as login problems or incorrect user information.
    \item To control access permissions for different user roles, such as students, lecturers, department leads, etc.
    \item To review feedback and reports submitted by users to improve the platform.
\end{itemize}
