\subsection*{User Story 3}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{User Story 3}
As a student who wants to get more involved on campus, I want to: 
\begin{itemize}
    \item View a list of clubs and societies that are available in my college
    \item Be able to sort the list according to my interests and preferences
    \item Register for clubs and societies through the app
    \item Get updates and notifications on meetings and events in the society
    \item Communicate with other members of the clubs and societies through the app
\end{itemize}

\subsection*{User Story 4}
\addcontentsline{toc}{subsection}{User Story 4}
As a student who finds it difficult to manage their time, I want to:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Have the ability to plan my tasks and classes
    \item Get reminders regarding future deadlines and exams
    \item Be able to set goals and monitor my progress towards them
    \item Have the ability to connect with other students for study groups and support
    \item Use the app to access resources and materials for my courses easily
\end{itemize}
